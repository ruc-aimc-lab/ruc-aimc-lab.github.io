We have two papers (one main track paper and one Findings track paper) accepted at [CVPR 2026](https://cvpr.thecvf.com/Conferences/2026). CVPR is the premier annual computer vision event comprising the main conference and several co-located workshops and short courses. With its high quality and low cost, it provides an exceptional value for students, academics and industry researchers.

1. Zhao *et al*., SAVE: Speech-Aware Video Representation Learning for Video-Text Retrieval. CVPR 2026
2. Wei *et al*., EI: Early Intervention for Multimodal Imaging based Disease Recognition. CVPR (Findings) 2026
